About me

I'm Jan, 21 years old. At the moment I am pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Communication Design. There I am learning various Design Concepts, Photography, traditional and digital Mediums, Softwares and create independent Projects with them. And to fill in the gaps I am learning some programming as well as Japanese in another campus and lead the 3D-Printing service in our School.

Since my childhood my peers regularly called me out for daydreaming. I have always been in my own creative headspace and world. Growing older and more mature I learned to be concentrated when I have to and creative when I need to. I love nature and the people around me, I love that we know so much and yet so little about the world, I love learning and growing as a Person. Everything inspires me to keep dreaming and creating.
